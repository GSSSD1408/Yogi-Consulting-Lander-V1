Why This Works

The research behind what we do.

Every pillar of our methodology is grounded in data. These aren't opinions — they're findings from the institutions that study operational performance at scale.

20–30% of revenue lost annually

Companies lose between a fifth and a third of their revenue every year to operational inefficiencies — broken processes, siloed systems, and manual workflows that compound as the business grows.

— IDC Market Research

7x more likely to qualify a lead

Companies that respond to inbound leads within one hour are seven times more likely to qualify them than those that wait even sixty minutes. Yet the average business takes 42+ hours to respond — and over 58% never respond at all.

— Harvard Business Review

20–30% productivity increase

Organizations that implement automation across their workflows see an average 20–30% increase in productivity. Two-thirds of surveyed companies also reported improvements in quality control, customer satisfaction, and reduced operating costs.

— McKinsey & Company

70% of transformations fail

Most digital transformations fail to meet their objectives — and Bain's research shows 88% fail to achieve their original ambitions. The common thread: technology deployed without first fixing the operational foundation underneath it.

— BCG, McKinsey & Bain
